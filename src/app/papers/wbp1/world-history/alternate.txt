This file is an archive of a history that I wrote, not realizing that it
had strayed too far from my original vision for the project.

<h2 id="the-age-of-true-unity">The Age of True Unity</h2>
<p>
  For almost 1,000 years, both the Humans and the Anthrans had cooperated.
  They had created great cities and kingdoms, as well as utilizing the <Link href="/papers/wbp1/the-arcoil">Arcoil</Link>{" "}
  to create infrastructure. During this time, the Arcoil had been utilized heavily,
  and knowledge of it was widespread.
</p>
<p>
  The end of this time period is marked by the Anthrans' plot to become the superior
  species on the planet. The Anthrans would begin a 40 year long war before finally
  claiming victory. The remaining Humans were subjugated and forced into slavery.
</p>
<h2 id="the-age-of-the-anthrans">The Age of the Anthrans</h2>
<p>
  The Anthrans, with the forced labor from the Humans, had prospered. Their civilization grew
  to having worldwide status. Use of the Arcoil was beginning to be moderated heavily by the
  elite, and was used to crack down on dissidence. Unauthorized use of the magic would be met
  with imprisonment and reeducation. 
</p>
<p>
  A group of rebellious Anthrans had formed that vowed to rescue the Humans from their current
  situation. They corralled every remaining Human and sent them to a planet in an alternate
  dimension. This planet would come to be known as "Earth" by the Humans. Every last member of
  this group was executed for treason against the Anthran regime.
</p>
<p>
  However, some Humans had remained, not trusting their saviors intentions. These Humans used
  the Arcoil to disguise themselves as Anthrans, and formed a sort of secret society so that they
  could live peacefully.
</p>
<p>
  Seeing as the backbone of Anthran civilization had just been shattered into dust, the inspiring
  story of the rebellious group had pushed the civilians to fight against their leaders. Now,
  the establishment was far too powerful to be taken on in a traditional sense. So, the new
  rebellion came up with a plan: to cause a full reset of society as they knew it. By combining
  the Arcoil of each and every person, they managed to reset the planet to a pre True Unity state,
  as well as erasing all knowledge of the Arcoil. This event, known as "The Expunging", marked
  the end of The Age of the Anthrans.
</p>
