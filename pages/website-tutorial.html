Getting Started

Have you ever wanted a space that is be completely yours? Somewhere where you can jot down your ideas and share them with whoever you want? With your own website, you can express your creativity with how it looks and what you decide to share on it. But, making a website is confusing. It can be hard to decide on where to even start learning. That is what this tutorial is for. This is a step by step guide on how to get started with making your own, personal website.
