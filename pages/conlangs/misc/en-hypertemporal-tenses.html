Hypertemporal Tenses

Introduction

The hypertemporal tenses are the hypertemporal past and hypertemporal future. Each is described below with their respective use cases

Hypertemporal future Used when talking about something in the present, as if you were in the future.
Hypertemporal past Used when talking about something in the present, as if you were in the past.
