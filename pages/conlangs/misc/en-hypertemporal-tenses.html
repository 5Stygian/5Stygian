Hypertemporal Tenses

Introduction

The hypertemporal tenses are the hypertemporal past, hypertemporal future, and hypertemporal past-future. Each is described below with their respective use cases

Hypertemporal future Used when talking about something in the present, as if you were in the future.
Hypertemporal past Used when talking about something in the present, as if you were in the past.
Hypertemporal past-future Use when talking about either the past or the future, as if you were from the opposite (when talking about the future, you use the past and vice versa).
