Super Chat Based Abbreviation Language

(SCBAL)

SCBAL (pronounced "see-ball") is designed to be used for democratic and efficient communication during missions in the game "Helldivers 2".

Sentence Structure

Sentances in SCBAL represent commands that players are meant to follow (eg. what objective to go to). There are many types of sentances in SCBAL, lets learn the most common one.
