Breaking Down the Phrases

"My name is..."

fill out later IPA Romanization Translation(s)
[rɒɛ̌ mɒ̌ɸɛ zǐʒːnɒː] rae mafe zijna My name is ...
The word "rae" translates to the first person nominative pronoun "I". The second word, "mafe", translates to "to have/I have". The infinitive and first person present conjugations of any verb are always the same. The final word, "zijna", translates to "name".
